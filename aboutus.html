About Us

We Are The Fine Print

The Fine Print is a nonprofit publishing organization dedicated to amplifying underrepresented voices in both creative writing and STEM fields. We believe that the most impactful stories and research often get overlooked, much like the small print we tend to miss. Our mission is to bring these "fine prints" to the forefront, showcasing the works of women, minorities, and young scientists whose contributions deserve a broader audience.

The Problem

Being heard is hard. Today, many talented writers and researchers from underrepresented backgrounds struggle to gain visibility and recognition for their work. These voices are often overlooked by traditional publishing, leading to a lack of diversity in storytelling and scientific writing. This marginalization results in a limited understanding of the world, as the narratives and insights from these diverse communities remain unheard.

Our Solution

The Fine Print aims to bridge this gap by providing a platform specifically designed for minorities groups. Through digitally publishing journals, articles, and collections, we highlight their work and their personal stories, ensuring they reach a wider audience. We also offer workshops, mentorship events, and more to empower these creators, helping them hone their skills and navigate the publishing landscape.

Impact

By amplifying underrepresented voices, The Fine Print fosters a richer, more inclusive narrative in both literature and STEM. Our initiatives not only elevate individual creators but also contribute to a broader cultural shift towards diversity in storytelling and research. We envision a future where all voices are celebrated, leading to innovative ideas, fresh perspectives, and a more equitable society.

What Was The Origin of The Fine Print?

Hi, everyone my name is Anya! I'm in 11th grade and from Virginia, USA! I founded The Fine Print as growing up I rarely saw diverse voices and perspectives reflected in literature, especially when going over class book syllabus's. It was disheartening to see so few works by women and people of color included in our studies. While reading a book I could never fully imagine my or a diverse voice as I didn't see those of different background portrayed in STEM or creative writing. Driven by a desire to change this, I started The Fine Print to highlight these important voices and offer a platform for underrepresented writers and young scientists. I am dedicated to bringing these often-overlooked stories and research to light, celebrating the richness and diversity that has been missing. With The Fine Print, I aim to make a significant, lasting difference and ensure that all voices have a chance to be heard. Join us in our mission to elevate and celebrate diverse contributions, one story at a time.

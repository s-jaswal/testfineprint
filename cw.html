Creative Writing Sector


The Writing Sector

Our main focus as The Fine Print's creative writing sector is to highlight the works and voices of women, minorities, and young writers. Our team will publish short stories, poetry, and other creative pieces, giving authors a platform to share their talent and receive the recognition they deserve.

Originality

We're looking for pieces that are unique and creative!

Passion

We're looking for pieces that emphasize your passion and love for writing. We want to publish authors who truly wish to reach a greater audience with works they spent time on.

Skill

We're looking for pieces that are organized, skillfully executed, and showcasing talent.
